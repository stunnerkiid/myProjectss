Successful Websites and Mobile Apps

Around the webpage you will see a number of websites and mobile applications. Creators are sometimes young innovative entrepreneurs in college like Mark Zuckerberg CEO of FaceBook. Mark started his social media website in his dorm room while attending Harvard University. While building and designing his website he realized that he had two choices one to devote all his time and efforts into his startup or stay in school. Many other application developers make simple and interactively addicting games such as flappy bird released in 2013. Flappy bird attracted many advertisers and made a lucrative amount of 40,000 a day.
